ALUMINIUM WINDOWS AND DOORS

ALUMINIUM WINDOWS AND DOORS:
We offer our clients varieties of aluminium doors and windows. These aluminium doors and windows are exclusive and economically beneficial for our clients and many more.

Our range of aluminium doors and windows are long lasting, very tough and strong as it is made of fine grade quality assured basic components.

Aluminium Domal Series Windows and Doors
We are dedicatedly involved in providing an excellent quality of JINDAL make Domal series Aluminium Windows and Doors for catering the various needs of our clients. We are offer wide range of wooden finish premium quality array of aluminium windows and Doors.

Aluminium Sliding Windows:
Sliding windows combines the strength and substance of an aluminium frame system with slim line mullion and transom profiles for maximised glass areas. Standard or lockable sash hardware can be selected. High quality sash weather seals improve weather tightness and with the use of optional glazing adaptors.

Aluiminum Sliding Doors:
The stylish sliding door solution designed with quality and functionality in mind, the features curved interlocks and high quality roller hardware to be one of the smoothest operating sliding doors in the market. It can cater for a wide range of applications. Its sturdy aluminium frame also accommodates integrated insect and security screening options.

Casement Windows:
Sliding window combines the strength and substance of an aluminium frame system with slim line mullion and transom profiles for maximised glass areas. Optional double glazing and the choice of either a flat face or period style sash allows the window to cater to a range of architectural styles and neatly integrated insect screening result in a sturdy and versatile window solution.

Double Hung Windows:
Double hung window combines the strength and substance of an aluminium frame system with a clean, flat profile design which blends easily with a range of architectural styles. Standard features such as full width integrated finger pulls on the upper and lower sashes aid ease of operations and key lockable are also available.

Louvers Window::
Designed specifically for residential & commercial applications, louver window offers a high level of weather performance with a slim line frame design. The louver window features high quality louver galleries for ease of operation and improved sealing. The aesthetics can be further enhanced by the unique aerofoil louver blade or a range of other glass or aluminium blade options.
